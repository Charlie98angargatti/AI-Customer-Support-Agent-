====================================================
  SHOPEASE E-COMMERCE REFUND & RETURN POLICY
  Effective Date: January 1, 2025
  Version: 3.3 (INR)
====================================================

1. ELIGIBILITY WINDOW
-----------------------
- Standard items: Refund or exchange requests must be submitted within 30 days of the
  confirmed delivery date.
- Electronics: 15 days from confirmed delivery date.
- Perishables / Food items: 3 days from confirmed delivery date.
- Digital products / Software licenses: NON-REFUNDABLE once the license key has been
  activated or the download link accessed.
- Personalized / custom-made items: NON-REFUNDABLE unless the item arrives damaged
  or defective. If damaged/defective, the standard 30-day window applies.

2. ITEM CONDITION REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------
- Items must be returned in original, unused condition with all original packaging,
  tags, and accessories intact.
- Items showing signs of use, wear, damage caused by the customer, or missing
  components will be rejected.
- A clear photo of the item and its condition must be submitted with the request.

3. VALID REFUND REASONS
--------------------------
APPROVED reasons (full refund):
  a) Item arrived damaged or defective (must report within 72 hours of delivery)
  b) Wrong item shipped (different from what was ordered)
  c) Item never arrived (must wait 10 business days past expected delivery)
  d) Significant product description mismatch (size, color, material)

APPROVED reasons (partial refund - 50%):
  e) Customer changed their mind — within the eligible return window and item unused
  f) Item does not fit / size issue — only for clothing and footwear category

DENIED reasons (no refund):
  g) Item was used and then returned ("buyer's remorse" after use)
  h) Damage caused by the customer (drops, spills, misuse)
  i) Request submitted after the eligibility window has expired
  j) Digital products with activated licenses
  k) Perishables reported after 3-day window
  l) Customer claims non-delivery but tracking confirms delivery and signature obtained
  m) Personalized/custom-made items, unless damaged or defective

4. ORDER VALUE LIMITS (INR)
------------------------------
- Orders under ₹800.00: No refund issued (non-refundable minimum threshold).
- Orders between ₹800.00 and ₹40,000.00: Standard refund process applies.
- Orders above ₹40,000.00: Require manager approval and may take up to 7 business days.
- Maximum single refund amount: ₹80,000.00 (amounts above require executive approval).

5. REFUND METHOD & TIMELINE
-----------------------------
- Original payment method refunds: 5–10 business days after approval.
- Store credit refunds: Issued within 24 hours of approval.
- Cash refunds for in-store purchases: Immediate upon approval.
- Shipping costs are NON-REFUNDABLE unless the return is due to our error (wrong item,
  defective item).

6. REPEAT / FRAUDULENT CLAIMS
-------------------------------
- Customers with more than 3 refund requests in a 90-day rolling window will be
  flagged for review. Refund may be denied pending investigation.
- Accounts suspected of refund fraud will be permanently banned.
- Any refund request with inconsistent information, fabricated damage claims, or
  mismatched order details will be denied and the account flagged.

7. SPECIAL CASES & EXCEPTIONS
-------------------------------
- Festive Season (Oct 1 – Jan 10): Eligible window extended to 45 days for standard
  items purchased as gifts.
- VIP / Premium Members: Eligible window extended by 15 additional days on all
  standard items.
- Subscription boxes: Only the most recent box is eligible for a refund claim.
- Bundle deals: The entire bundle must be returned; partial bundle refunds are not
  accepted.

8. HOW TO SUBMIT A CLAIM
--------------------------
Customer must provide:
  1. Order ID
  2. Full name on the account
  3. Reason for refund (from the approved list above)
  4. Photo evidence (for damaged/defective items)
  5. Preferred refund method

Claims are reviewed within 2–3 business days.

9. ESCALATION
--------------
If a customer disagrees with a refund decision, they may escalate by emailing
support@shopease.com with subject line "REFUND ESCALATION – [Order ID]".
An escalation team will respond within 5 business days.
